Build Your Career: Work Opportunities in Germany

Earn While You Learn: Flexible Part-Time Opportunities

International students from non-EEA countries have excellent opportunities to gain work experience. You're permitted to work for either **140 full full working days or 280 half working days per year**. This count excludes holidays and sick days, giving you ample flexibility. Alternatively, you can work up to **20 hours per week** during academic terms, allowing you to manage your studies and finances effectively.

Your Future in Germany: Post-Study Work Visa Pathways

After successfully completing your studies, Germany welcomes you to stay and contribute! Students are eligible for an **18-month Job Seeking Visa** (often referred to as a post-study work visa). We highly recommend starting your job hunt during your final semester, as this crucial extended work period begins immediately after you receive your final exam results. Pave your way to a thriving career in Germany!
